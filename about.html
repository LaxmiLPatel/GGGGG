GEMINI
Multi-user Dialogs with Google Gemini

Welcome to this website made for the Google AI Hackathon 2024. Lalit and Laxmi Patel have attempted here to evaluate and demonstrate the power and usefulness of Google's Gemini AI platform.

The Gemenu page contains a multi-user chatbot for Google Gemini to advise the user what to order from food avaiable to suit the user's dietary restrictions and health issues. This can be very useful to individuals and restaurants.

The Gemedi page contains a multi-user chatbot for Google Gemini to alert the user about possible medicinal complications according to user's health and eating. This can be very useful to individuals and pharmacies.

The Gemtax page contains a multi-user chatbot for Google Gemini to answer questions pertaining to the user's tax jurisdiction and tax type. This can be very useful to individuals and tax offices.

The Gemlla page contains a multi-user chatbot for Google Gemini and Meta Llama to respond to an input. This can be very useful for comparing Gemini with Llama.
